1. Pitch Meetings

Ryan George does videos through Screen Rant on Youtube where he acts as the person pitching movies to a producer. He plays both parts, and makes fun of different movies and how the plots work through his "pitch".

He has various catchphrases that are a part of each video such as:

  • Super easy, barely an inconvenience
  • Describing anything as "tight"
  • Whoops, Whoopsie
  • Yeah, yeah,yeah!

These will make more sense, of course, if you watch some of the videos! I have provided a link to a more recent one: the pitch meeting for Twilight, as well as link to more pitch meetings if you want to see more!

3. Faceswap Videos

Faceswap videos are so strange. I love them because they always leave me wondering "What did I just watch?" However, even with how weird they are, I cannot stop laughing and I always want to watch them again. I have two favorites.

The first one is a dad who puts his face on his daughters. It is so unsettling and hilarious at the same time. It is also a video that is now frequently quoted in my house. The video I have posted a link to is actually one of several that this dad has created. The Channel on Youtube is called Woodsie TV.

Dad swaps with daughters-Food Critic

My other favorite faceswap video is for those who know Nick Offerman (Ron Swanson) from Parks and Recreation, and Full House. A video was made of the opening title sequence of full house, where all the characters have Ron Swanson's face.
