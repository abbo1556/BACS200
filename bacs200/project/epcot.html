Eating Around The World!

Many people who visit Epcot “drink around the world”. That is not really my thing. My second semester working for Disney, my now husband and I, spent the semester eating around the world instead! There are 11 countries total. We skipped America though so we went to the 10 foreign countries. We started in Mexico and ended in Canada. It was so much fun! It gave us date ideas through the entire semester, and of course it was fun to complete our mission by the end! My favorite country to eat in was China. My favorite experience was probably Norway, since it is a character meal and you get to meet a lot of the princesses!
