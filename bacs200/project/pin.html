Pin Trading

In 1999, Disney introduced pin trading! All over Disney property, you can purchase pins that go on a lanyard. You can get specific individual pins or trading sets. Many of the cast members wear pin lanyards and you can trade your pins with them! You also can trade with other guests if you both want to.

I have been pin trading since I was 12. I thought I would outgrow it, but I never did! Now my collection is bigger than ever. I started off collecting 101 dalmatians pins, but over the years I started lots of different collections.

Now some of the ones I love to collect include Tangled, Up, Lady and the Tramp, Moana, and Disney rides. I also just love finding cute ones in general!

There are all different kinds of pins, from characters to rides, hidden mickeys and limited editions. Now when I go to the parks, I know what my souvenirs will always be. More recently, you can purchase some pins on the Disney website as well.
