What do I Find Amusing?

1. Pitch Meetings

Ryan George does videos through Screen Rant on Youtube where he acts as the person pitching movies to a producer. He plays both parts, and makes fun of different movies and how the plots work through his "pitch".

He has various catchphrases that a part of every video such as:

  • Super easy, barely an inconvenience
  • Describing anything as "tight"
  • Whoops, Whoopsie
  • Yeah, yeah,yeah!

These will make more sense, of course, if you watch some of the videos! I have provided a link to a more recent one: the pitch meeting for Twilight, as well as link to more pitch meetings if you want to see more!
